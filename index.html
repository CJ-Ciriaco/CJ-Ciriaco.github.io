About Our Group

We are a team of Senior High School researchers from the University of Santo Tomas, committed to producing meaningful, data-driven work that contributes to social change.

🎯 Our Mission

To conduct rigorous, socially relevant research that addresses real-world problems in our community, and to communicate our findings through accessible digital platforms.

🌟 Our Vision

To be a model of collaborative, innovative, and academically excellent research within the UST Senior High School community and beyond.

🎨 Logo Rationale

The "CH" monogram represents our group's identity as Group 4 — a central gathering point for ideas, data, and advocacy. The circular green form symbolizes growth, renewal, and the positive change our research aspires to create. The gradient into blue reflects trust, depth, and the digital nature of our platform. Every element was deliberately chosen to reflect our commitment to collaboration, innovation, and the pursuit of meaningful change through research.
